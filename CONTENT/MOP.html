Manila Ocean Park

Manila Ocean Park is more than just a theme park – it’s an adventure playground for all ages! With attractions for kids, teens, and adults alike, this wonderful marine theme park and premiere educational grounds will be an unforgettable experience for everyone. From the moment you arrive, you’ll be creating great memories, learning about the world’s wildlife, and spending quality time with your loved ones.

First up is the massive Oceanarium which houses 14,000 sea animals, all of which are native to the Philippines and Southeast Asia. Manila Ocean Park is one of the world’s biggest public aquariums so you’re bound to see some of the most majestic creatures swimming overhead. Although there aren’t any singing whales to give you a Manila concert experience, feel free to say hi to the sharks and stingrays as you make your way through the breathtaking underwater tunnel.

You don’t need to travel to Antarctica to see penguins up close because Manila Ocean Park has its very own penguin park facility. It’s the first in the country and gives you a glimpse of what it's like on the coldest continent in the world. Aside from meeting the adorable Humboldt penguins, visitors get to learn more about Antarctica and get merry at the Christmas Village located in this exhibit. You will be able to see the world of creepy crawlies, all-star bird shows, sea lion show, jellies exhibit, super toy collection, and the dining view
